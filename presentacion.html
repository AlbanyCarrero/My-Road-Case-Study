Frontend Project

MY ROAD

Albany Carrero

Goal and theme of the website

Travel website

It is a website aimed at providing guidance and information for people wishing to make and plan road trips, offering information on routes, accommodation, documentation and more.

Target audience

The target audience is:

  • Genre: Indifferent (female, male, other).
  • Age: 25 to 35 years old
  • Socio-economic level: Medium, medium-high
  • Lifestyle: Adventurous and outgoing.

Information Architecture

Sitemap

The sitemap was developed in accordance with the project's goals and taking into consideration its particular requirements. This approach facilitated the visual and consistent representation of the content hierarchy and interaction, as well as incorporating essential features to ensure functionality.

Wireframes

Desktop

Mid-fidelity

I developed the Wireframes models for desktop inspired by the organisation of other similar travel websites. This allowed me to focus on visual consistency and hierarchy before defining styles and starting to develop the site at the code level.

Mobile

Mid-fidelity

As well as designing the wireframes for the desktop version, I also created wireframes for the mobile version. This allowed me to visualise and plan how the site's content would adapt on different devices. However, as the project progressed, the organisation of these elements changed.
